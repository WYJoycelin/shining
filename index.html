Our Story

It is a pleasure to introduce Mao House. Mao House is not only a bed and breakfast accommodation, but also the landmark building of Yu-Guan Island. Two years ago, Dr. Huang Sen Chuan took me to Yu-Guan Island, then I bought an eighteen squares land. Now, our three-floor house is successively developed into completion of the first stage of Mao House. I would like to specially thank Dr. Huang imparted his knowledge to Mao House development.

Today, Shangri-La in my dream appears in Yu-Guan Island which includes historic bridge, designer house, and local culture. I am devoted in architecture studying with innovating new style building. For many years, I have set up this kind of distinguished building culture that merges modern and traditional culture. In the era of construction transition, we have to integrate our traditional culture with modern technique in order to keep our memory and emotion into the building. We believed Mao House will be the future symbol in this historic era.

Mao House combined Taiwanese and Tainan local culture, together which will be continued and perpetuity forever to influence ideas and spirits of people. My life, memory and dream came alive when building Mao House. We hope Taiwan and Tainan will become famous tourist spots in the world one day. The best place to start Tainan cultural adventure is from Yu-Guan Island.

我們的故事

很高興向您介紹毛家大院。毛宅不僅是一家民宿，也是玉關島的標志性建築。兩年前，黃森傳博士帶我來到漁官島，然後我買下了一塊十八平方的土地。現在，我們的三層樓房已陸續開發完成，"茅屋 "一期工程也已竣工。在此，我要特別感謝黃博士為毛房子的開發所傳授的知識。

今天，我夢中的香格裡拉出現在玉關島，這里有歷史悠久的橋梁、設計師的房子和當地的文化。多年來，我建立了這種融合現代與傳統文化的傑出建築文化。在建築轉型的時代，我們必須將傳統文化與現代技術相結合，將我們的記憶和情感融入建築之中。我們相信，在這個歷史性的時代，"毛房子 "將成為未來的象徵。

毛房子結合了台灣文化和臺南地方文化，將永遠延續下去，影響人們的思想和精神。我的生命、記憶和夢想在建造毛家大屋的過程中得以實現。我們希望有一天，台灣和臺南能成為世界著名的旅游勝地。臺南文化探險的最佳起點是從玉觀音島開始的。
